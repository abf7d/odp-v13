These visualizations:
  • 1 are intended to provide an at-a-glance summary of the in vitro activity of therapeutics against individual SARS-CoV-2 variants and reference strains (What are in vitro studies?)
  • 2 should not be used to conduct side-by-side comparisons of therapeutics   (Why?)
  • 3 show data collected from in vitro assays, and it is not known how in vitro neutralization assay data correlate with clinical outcomes  (What does this mean?)
  • 4 may show data from preliminary reports that have not been peer reviewed and thus the data: (What does this mean?)
    • should not be regarded as conclusive
    • should not guide clinical practice or health decisions
    • should not be reported in news media as established information
